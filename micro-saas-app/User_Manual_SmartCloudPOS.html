InstaMunim

- Beyond Billing - Complete User Manual

Welcome to InstaMunim! This manual will guide you step-by-step through every single feature of the application, from logging in to tracking your daily net profit and marketing to your customers via WhatsApp.

1. Getting Started & Security

Logging In

Open the app. You will be greeted by the secure login screen.

  1. Enter your registered Mobile Number.
  2. Enter your Password. You can tap the Eye Icon to reveal the password while typing to avoid mistakes.
  3. Tap Login. The app will remember you for future sessions.

App Navigation & Exit Protection

Use the bottom navigation bar to switch between Dashboard, Cart, Customers, and Settings.

  • Safe Back Button: If you are in Settings or Cart and press your phone's back button, the app will safely take you back to the Dashboard.
  • Exit Confirmation: If you press back on the Dashboard, the app will ask "Are you sure you want to exit?" to prevent you from accidentally closing the app during billing.

2. Billing & The Smart Voice Cashier

This app features an advanced Voice Assistant that acts like a digital cashier, allowing you to bill customers hands-free.

How to use Voice Billing (Mic Button)

1. Tap the big red/orange Microphone icon on the Dashboard.
2. The app will start listening continuously.
3. Simply speak the customer's order. Example: "Ek chicken burger lagao, do momos aur ek pepsi."
4. You can also say the customer's name and number: "Naam Rahul hai, number 9876543210."
5. The app will automatically add these items to the cart and save the customer's details!

The Voice Assistant is continuous. If you pause to take money, it will automatically restart listening without you having to press the mic button again. Press the red mic button again to manually stop it.

3. Cart, Checkout, and Change Calculation

Once items are added to the cart (manually by tapping or via Voice), you proceed to checkout.

  1. Review the items in the cart. You can increase/decrease quantities using the + and - buttons.
  2. Select the payment method: Cash, Digital (UPI), or Udhaar (Credit).
  3. Cash Entry Feature: If the customer gives you a ₹500 note for a ₹120 bill, tap on the "Cash Given" field and enter 500. The app will instantly show you exactly how much Change (₹380) you need to return.
  4. Tap Complete Order.

4. WhatsApp Invoicing & Receipts

Sending Bills via WhatsApp

Stop printing paper bills! After an order is completed, a success screen appears.

  • If a customer mobile number was provided, tap the Send WhatsApp Receipt button.
  • WhatsApp will automatically open with a beautifully formatted text bill containing your Shop Name, Items, Prices, and Total.
  • Just press send!

5. Financial Analytics & Dashboard Tracking

Sales, Expenses & Net Profit

The top of your Dashboard shows your daily business health in real-time.

  • Gross Sales: Total revenue generated today.
  • Aggregator Commissions: Automatic deductions for Swiggy/Zomato orders.
  • Daily Expenses: Tap 'Add Expense' to log things like raw material costs or electricity.
  • Net Profit: The most important number. Automatically calculated as: (Gross Sales - Commissions - Expenses).

Pull-to-Refresh Syncing

Your data is backed up to the cloud. To ensure you have the latest data (especially if you use the app on multiple devices):

Go to the Dashboard, touch the top of the screen, and Swipe Down. A loading spinner will appear, and your sales, expenses, and Udhaar data will instantly sync with the cloud database.

6. Udhaar (Credit) Management

Managing informal credit is crucial for local businesses. The app handles this automatically.

How to manage Udhaar

1. During Checkout, select Udhaar as the payment method.
2. Go to the Customers tab from the bottom menu.
3. You will see a list of all customers who owe you money, along with their total outstanding balance.
4. When a customer comes to pay their debt, find their name and tap the Mark as Paid button.
5. The balance clears to zero, and the money is added to today's Sales!

7. WhatsApp Promotional Marketing

Turn your customer database into a marketing tool to increase repeat sales.

  • Navigate to the Marketing tab.
  • Here, you can type a custom promotional message or choose from templates (e.g., "Get 20% off this Sunday!" or "Happy Diwali from our shop!").
  • The app uses the customer phone numbers you saved during billing.
  • Clicking send will allow you to broadcast these offers directly to your customers' WhatsApp, acting as a free mini-CRM system.

8. Settings & Customization

Make the app truly yours by configuring the settings.

Personalizing Your Shop

  • Business Profile: Update your Shop Name, Owner Name, and Contact info. This name appears on all WhatsApp bills.
  • Logo Upload: Upload a picture of your shop's logo to personalize the app's interface.
  • Cloud Status: View the last time your data was successfully backed up to the secure cloud.
  • Logout: Securely sign out of your device.
