Beyond Billing - User Guide v1.1

1. Getting Started

Welcome to InstaMunim! This app is designed to make your shop billing and accounting fast and simple. You can manage everything from your smartphone.

Download & Install

Download the InstaMunim APK file from the official link and install it on your Android phone.

7-Day Free Trial

Every new store gets a 7-day full access trial. You can start billing immediately after registration.

2. Smart Billing Features

Create professional bills for your customers in seconds.

Quick Invoicing

Search products by name or SKU and add them to the cart instantly.

GST & Non-GST Billing

Option to create bills with GST or simple bills without GST.

Digital Receipts

Generate digital receipts that you can share via WhatsApp or print using a Bluetooth printer.

Return Management

Easily manage returned items and adjust stock automatically.

3. Advanced Inventory

Keep track of your products and never run out of stock.

Stock Tracker

View real-time stock levels. The app notifies you when stock is low.

Bulk Import

Add multiple products at once to save time.

Multiple Categories

Organize products into categories for faster searching.

Profit Margin Set

Set cost price and selling price to track your actual profit on every item.

4. Complete Accounting

Manage your expenses and shop costs without a calculator.

Expense Manager

Record daily shop expenses like tea, cleaning, electricity, etc.

Rent Target Tracker

Set your monthly rent target. The app shows you how much daily sales you need to cover your rent.

Profit/Loss Analytics

Clear charts showing your total sales vs total expenses every month.

Tax Reports

Download simple tax summaries for your CA or accountant.

5. Staff & Security

Add staff members and keep your data safe.

Staff Roles

Add staff members with limited permissions. They can create bills but cannot see your total profits or edit sensitive settings.

Automated Cloud Backup

Your data is automatically synced to the cloud. Even if you lose your phone, your data is safe.

6. Store Settings

Customize the app for your brand.

  • Logo Upload: Add your shop logo to the bills.
  • Header/Footer: Add your address, phone number, and a "Thank You" message to every bill.
  • GSTIN: Save your GST number to generate valid tax invoices.

PREMIUM Send automated messages to customers after billing to increase repeat visits. Share special offers and festive greetings directly from the app.
