# Project: Restaurant Micro-SaaS (MVP)
## Current Status: Professional MVP Ready

### 1. Project Vision & Soul (Project ki Rooh)
- **The Mission:** This isn't just a billing app; it's an empowerment tool for the common man. The goal is to provide enterprise-level technology to street food vendors who are often ignored by big tech.
- **The Emotion:** To give every "Thela" or "Stall" owner the pride of running a digital business. We focus on "Dignity" and "Growth".
- **The Founder's Passion:** Zainul Khan (a QA expert) is building this to solve real-world struggles of small businesses. The app must be as simple as a "Voice Message" but as powerful as "Petpooja".
- **User Tone:** The app uses Hinglish and local concepts (like Rent Tracker) because it understands the Indian street food culture.

### 2. Core Features Implemented:
- **Voice POS (Killer Feature):** Supports Hinglish voice commands to add items, quantities, and payment types (Cash/Online/Udhaar).
- **Hinglish AI Logic:** Can extract customer names and 10-digit mobile numbers from natural speech.
- **Rent Tracker:** Psychological motivation tool that calculates daily profit margins to show progress toward monthly rent goals.
- **WhatsApp Invoicing:** Automated bills sent via WhatsApp "Click-to-Chat" to save costs and stay compliant.
- **Thermal Printing:** Optimized receipt layout (58mm) with an Auto-Print toggle in settings.
- **Platform Analytics:** Tracks Swiggy/Zomato sales and automatically deducts commissions to show "Net Profit (Bachhat)".
- **Smart CRM:** One-click WhatsApp marketing for returning/losing customers.
- **Legal & Privacy:** Full Terms & Conditions and Disclaimers integrated for commercial safety.
- **Help & Support:** Direct contact details for the founder (Zainul Khan).

### 3. Tech Stack:
- **Frontend:** Next.js (React), Tailwind CSS.
- **Icons:** Lucide React.
- **Mobile-Ready:** Optimized for Android & iOS (Responsive + Touch-friendly).
- **Architecture:** Prepared for Capacitor (Native APK/IPA) and Supabase (Backend).
